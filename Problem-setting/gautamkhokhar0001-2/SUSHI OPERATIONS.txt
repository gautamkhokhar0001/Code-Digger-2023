Since she doesnt know about multiplication and divisions create a programme for her without using '*'or'/' to complete the task.You can use addition and subtraction and other operations.

Input:
Let there be t test cases. 
Input each number

Output:
1)If number when divided by 2 yields 0.75 as decimal part then print "YES"
2)if it doesn't yields 0.75,then print"NO".

Constraints:
0<t<1e5 
0<n<1e5
